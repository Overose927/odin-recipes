Arroz con Pollo

Discription

Most of my favorite foods growing up was rice. With the uncanny ability to take on any flavor that was cooked with it that made it never boring to eat. Amongst my favorite rice dishes is Arroz con Pollo.

I have eaten it more than I would like to admit but it never gets boring with its nutty flavor and enticing aroma, it will be the center piece of any mexican food night.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of bone-in skinless chicken rear quarters
  • Adobo seasoning
  • 3 cups of Jasmine Rice
  • 2 TBSPs of tomato flavored chicken Bouillon
  • 2 packets of Sazon culantro y achiote
  • 2 14.5 ounce cans of Pigeon Peas.
  • 10 ounces of frozen mixed vegatables.
  • Salt and pepper to taste
